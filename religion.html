Catholic and Orthodox

Catholic actually comes from the word universal, so what it means is universal Christianity. Orthodox relates to following the correct beliefs according to tradition, religion, and law. The roman catholic church is considered to be the center of catholicism.

The orthodox catholic church has no central doctrinal or governmental authority. It considers Jesus Christ to be the head of the church and religion.

Orthodox belief holds that the Orthodox Church is Christianity's true, holy, and apostolic church, tracing its origin directly to the institution established by Jesus Christ.
